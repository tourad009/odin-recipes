Simple Chicken Brine

Description

This simple chicken brine will help make the meat more tender and juicy.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Salt
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Pour warm water into a container
  2. Add salt, soy sauce and olive oil
  3. Stir until sugar and salt have dissolved
  4. Allow brine to cool to room temperature
